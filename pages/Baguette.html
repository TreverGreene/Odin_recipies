Baguette

Description

This recipe is mostly taken from king arthur flour's wonderful baguette recipe, but also incorporates elements from America's Test Kitchen: Bread Illustrated. The Baguettes come out crispy on the outside and wonderfully soft on the inside, a treat on their own or, in my ideal, with a soft cheese.

Time to completion: 36 hours

Ingredients

  • X Cups Bread Flour
